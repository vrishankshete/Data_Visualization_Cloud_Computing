    Visualizations help people see things that were not obvious to them before. Even when data volumes are very large, patterns can be spotted quickly and easily. Visualizations convey information in a universal manner and make it simple to share ideas with others. Graph Visualization

Our list of Graph Visualization.

Data visualization is both an art and a science.
"Main goal of data visualization is to communicate information clearly and effectively through graphical means, i.e GRAPHS and CHARTS. To convey ideas effectively, visualization helps users in analyzing and reasoning about data. It makes complex data more accessible, understandable and usable. Users may have particular analytical tasks, such as making comparisons or understanding causality, and the design principle of the graphic charts of various types are used to show patterns or relationships in the data for one or more variables."

Line Chart

The line chart is represented by a series of datapoints connected with a straight line. Line charts are most often used to visualize data that changes over time.

Area Chart

The area chart functions the same way as a line chart only it fills the area between the line and the threshold, which is 0 by default.

Column & Bar Chart

A column chart displays data as vertical bars. The bar chart is exactly the same as a column chart only the x-axis and y-axis are switched.

Pie Chart

A pie chart is a circular chart divided into sectors which is proportional to the quantity it represents.

Scatter Chart

A scatter chart draw a single point for each point of data in a series without connecting them.

More Categories

Includes Bubble charts, 3D-plots, Spider Web etc. However, we focused more on first five categories.

Our Work Process.

Stage 1: Explore.

Compliment to data.gov.in;
Take JSON format and plot the charts of different kinds;
publish the charts on a site.

Stage 2: Design.

Initially, we start with matplotlib (a python library). We got charts, but while publishing we cannot gather much information from them, as they were static images. So we have to find some other to solve this visualization problem.

Stage 3: Final Design

We made our final charts using HIGHCHARTS, as they provide sufficient information to understand the scenario about the situation. Additionaly, these charts provide interactive as per events.
